CALM ZONE

Helps you know more about Mental Health

1. General Stress and Anxiety-
Mindful Breathing (Box Breathing):
Inhale for a count of 4, hold for 4, exhale for 4, and pause for 4.

Guided Imagery Meditation:
Visualize a peaceful scene, focusing on details like colors, sounds, and sensations.

2. Depression-
Loving-Kindness Meditation (Metta):
Send positive thoughts and well-wishes to oneself and others.

Diaphragmatic Breathing:
Focus on deep, slow breaths expanding the diaphragm.

3. Panic Attacks-
Grounding Techniques:
The 5-4-3-2-1 method, identifying and focusing on five things you can see, four things you can touch, three things you can hear, two things you can smell, and one thing you can taste.

Square Breathing:
Inhale, hold, exhale, hold - each for a count of 4.

4. Insomnia-
Progressive Muscle Relaxation (PMR):
Tense and release muscle groups starting from toes to head.

4-7-8 Breathing:
Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 7, exhale for 8.
